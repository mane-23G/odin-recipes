Chicken Tacos

Description

This recipe is simple but delicous! The slow cooked seasoned chicken is the star of the show. This recipe was passed down for generation in my family. This recipe has been in my family since 2022. I hope this becomes a staple in your rotation of the 5 foods you eat in a week! :)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, bonless chicken breast

Steps

  1. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.
  3. Cook on LOW for 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken.
