Mac and Chees

Description

This recipe is simple but delicous! The slow cooked mac and cheese serves for a simple summer meal or a quick after work dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded cheedar cheese
  • 1 (5 ounce) can evaported milke
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condednsed cheddare cheese soup
  • 1 pinch paprika

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and a bit of salt and bring it to a boil. Cook macaroni into the pot and cook for 5 minutes. Drain and transfer into the slow cooker.
  2. Add butter to pasta and stir until it melts, season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle cheddar cheese over pasta and stir.
  3. In a separate bowl mix the evaported milk and eggs together until smooth, then stir into the pasta mixture.
  4. In the same bowl whisk the milk and condednsed soup together, and then mix into the pasta.
  5. Sprinkle the remaning cheese into the pasta mixture and garnish with paprika.
  6. Cook on LOW for 3 hours.
