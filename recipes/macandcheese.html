Mac and Chees

Description

This recipe is simple but delicous! The slow cooked mac and cheese serves for a simple summer meal or a quick after work dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded cheedar cheese
  • 1 (5 ounce) can evaported milke
  • 2 eggs, beaten
