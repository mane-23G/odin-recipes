Sauasage with Sauce

Description

This recipe is simple but delicous! The slow cooked sauasge is a quick and simple meal perfect for lunch time!

Ingredients

  • 8 (4 ounce) linkes fresh Italian sausage
  • 1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 green bell pepper sliced into strips
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Steps

  1. Place Italian sausage, spaghtetti sauce, green pepper, and onion into the slow cooker and mix until well combined.
  2. Cook on LOW for 6.
  3. Serve on the hoagie.
